The NDTwin research, development, and maintenance team is formed and led by Prof. Shie-Yuan Wang of National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (also affiliated with Academia Sinica since November 2024 until now).

The team members of NDTwin are mainly from Research Center for Information Technology Innovation, Academia Sinica, and Department of Computer Science, National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University.
